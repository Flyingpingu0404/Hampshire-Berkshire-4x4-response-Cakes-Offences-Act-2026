1. Lateness

(1) A Responder commits an offence if they fail to arrive at a designated muster point, briefing room, event coordination desk, or active shift deployment at or before the exact time specified by the coordinator.
(2) The standard grace period shall be precisely zero (0) minutes. Any arrival past the scheduled transmission timestamp constitutes an immediate breach of shift harmony.
(3) The penalty for standard lateness shall be determined as follows:
(a) Lateness under 15 minutes: Provision of minor confectioneries at the next shift gap, scaling to one per person present;
(b) Lateness exceeding 15 minutes: Upgrade to a mandatory full-sized tea-room sharing cake or individual standard treats scaling to one per person present;
(c) Lateness to an active logistics layout or extreme weather emergency: Immediate escalation to premium bakery items, scaling strictly to one per person present.
