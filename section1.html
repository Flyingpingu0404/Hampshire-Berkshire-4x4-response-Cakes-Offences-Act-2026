1. Short Title, Scope, and Application

(1) This Act may be cited for all official and unofficial purposes as the Hampshire & Berkshire 4x4 Response Cakes (Offences) Act 2026.
(2) This Act shall apply directly to all active operational drivers, control room staff, logistics handlers, supervisors, and volunteers, hereinafter legally referred to as "the Responders."
(3) Seniority or management rank provides no immunity under this Act. Rather, any offence committed by an officer of supervisory or coordinator rank shall be subjected to immediate Rank Aggravation, automatically multiplying the required culinary penalty volume by a factor of 2.
(4) This Act governs any action, communication, or omission occurring during active deployments, standby shifts, charity event coordination, training exercises, or while utilizing any team communications infrastructure.
