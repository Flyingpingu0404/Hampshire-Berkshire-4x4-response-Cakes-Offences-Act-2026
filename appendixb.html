APPENDIX B

Cakes can not be banked. Any 'kindhearted' purchase of cakes shall not be taken into consideration for the purposes of mitigating any offences committed in the future.
Reduced price cakes are not accepted under any circumstances as cakes resulting from an offence. Any attempt to do so shall be considered a further offence under Section 22 of this Act.
