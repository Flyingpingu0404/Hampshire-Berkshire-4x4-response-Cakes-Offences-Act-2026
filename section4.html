4. Work Birthday/Anniversary Cakes

(1) Upon a Responder achieving a major milestone of volunteer service or anniversary with the unit (including but not limited to 1 year, 5 years, or 10 years of active service), a statutory cake obligation is triggered.
(2) Unlike a standard personal birthday, responsibility for the procurement of a Work Anniversary Cake shall follow a collaborative layout:
(a) For standard annual milestones, the individual Responder shall bring in a selection of goods to celebrate their ongoing survival and commitment to the unit, scaling strictly to exactly one item per person present;
(b) For significant multi-year milestones (5+ years), the immediate team or regional coordinator shall assist in providing a ceremonial pastry asset scaling to the full headcount to honor the dedication shown.
(3) Fulfilling a service anniversary cake requirement provides temporary legal immunity from standard low-level banter for a period of exactly twenty-four (24) hours following consumption.
(4) If a service milestone passes completely uncelebrated due to logistical oversight, any member of the team may file an emergency motion with the Court of Kettle Appeal to mandate a retrospective delivery on the very next training or deployment cycle, scaling to one premium treat per person present.
