8. Return from Extended Absence / Milestone Trips

(1) The unit explicitly recognizes that all Responders are dedicated volunteers who give their time freely and generously around personal, family, and professional commitments. Consequently, standard unavailability or routine rota sign-offs carry zero culinary liability.
(2) **The Celebration of Return Framework:** A voluntary cake milestone is instead established when a Responder returns to active status following a major personal milestone trip, an international expedition, or an extended sabbatical, serving as an operational "catch-up" gesture for the team break room.
(3) **The Sunshine Bragging Clause:** Where a volunteer returns from an exceptionally luxurious or enviable tropical location and chooses to share photographic evidence of beaches, pools, or clear blue skies in the team **WhatsApp** channel, a voluntary "Sunshine Sharing Tier" is unlocked:
(a) **Voluntary Souvenir Treats:** The returning volunteer may optionally bring in regional delicacies or unique sweets from their travels, scaling strictly to one item per person present; or
(b) **The Heavy Rain Mitigation:** If the team was actively deploying in mud, severe weather, or flood conditions while the photos were being transmitted, the returnee is strongly encouraged to boost team morale with premium bakery doughnuts scaling to exactly one per person present.
(4) For the absolute avoidance of doubt, no automated system flags or enforcement citations shall ever be issued under this section. The provision of baked goods here is powered entirely by camaraderie, vacation stories, and mutual respect for the unit's collective sweet tooth.
