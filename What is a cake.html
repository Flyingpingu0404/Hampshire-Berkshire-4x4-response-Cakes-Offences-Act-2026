APPENDIX A

For the purposes of the standard cake fine, it should be noted that 'Jaffa Cakes' either branded as such or differently, are not cakes for the purposes of this Act.
Cakes is understood to include any of the following; this list is not exhaustive—
(1) Cakes
(2) Muffins
(3) Cookies
(4) Doughnuts
(5) Slices
Cakes is understood to include any of the following in the instance of an aggravated offence—
(1) Krispy Creme Doughnuts
(2) Cream Cakes
(3) Homemade / freshly baked cakes
(4) An overwhelming number of 'standard cakes' resulting in at least two each.
Under Section 8, cakes as defined above can replaced with exciting treat from a holiday destination.
