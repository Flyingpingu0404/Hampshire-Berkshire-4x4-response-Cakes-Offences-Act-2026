3. Birthday Cakes

(1) Every active Responder has a statutory obligation to provide a high-quality cake to the team break room or staging area on, or during the immediate shift following, the precise anniversary of their birth.
(2) For the avoidance of doubt, the celebration of a birthday does not grant the individual the right to receive free baked goods from others. Under this Act, the burden of culinary provision rests entirely on the person turning a year older.
(3) It is a strict offence to attempt a "Birthday Stealth Protocol," defined as trying to quietly pass through a shift or deployment cycle without informing the team of the birthday in order to avoid buying cake.
(4) If a Birthday Stealth Protocol is discovered by any peer via social media, official databases, or casual conversation, the Court of Kettle Appeal shall immediately issue an aggravated penalty notice requiring double the standard volume of sugary goods.
