6. Emergency / Red Button / Man Down Button Cakes

(1) A Responder commits a severe operational offence if they accidentally, negligently, or catastrophically trigger a false alarm or emergency alert across any part of the unit's communications infrastructure.
(2) For the purposes of subsection (1), a "False Alarm Communications Infraction" is explicitly made out by any of the following occurrences:
(a) **The Hardware Misstep:** Depressing the dedicated orange or red emergency button on a mobile vehicle transceiver or handheld digital radio terminal;
(b) **The Gravitational Fail (Man Down):** Triggering an automated tilt, motion, or "Man Down" sensor alarm because a radio terminal was left upside down, dropped on a seat, or worn awkwardly while a Responder is taking a nap;
(c) **The Digital Distress Trigger:** Accidentally activating an emergency status, critical alert, or "SOS" toggle within the **Responder Manager** control software; or
(d) **The Zello or Network Broadcast Panic:** Accidentally triggering an alert or open-mic transmission on **Zello**, or dropping an alarming, out-of-context message into an official team **WhatsApp** broadcast or chat group that forces controllers to investigate.
(3) An offence under this section is established the exact moment control room operators, shift managers, or regional team monitors are forced to halt standard operational workflow to run an emergency safety check on the unit, only to receive a sheepish apology back.
(4) Because halting emergency communications compromises unit tracking, violations are classified as **High-Tier Pastry Infractions** subject to the following penalty parameters:
(a) Alerts cleared and neutralized within 10 seconds: One (1) standard cake or treat per person present to clear the net error;
(b) Alerts that trigger controller callbacks or radio channel lockdown before being cleared: Premium bakery-fresh doughnuts scaling strictly to exactly one per person present; or
(c) Any false alarm that causes a secondary response vehicle to actively set off to check on the unit: Immediate escalation to a premium multi-item bakery selection scaling strictly to a minimum of two treats per person present.
(5) Legitimate emergency network activations where an actual threat to life, vehicle recovery emergency, or major hazard exists are completely exempt from this section.
