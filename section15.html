15. Turning Up in a Car Cakes

(1) A Responder triggers an immediate and unappealable vehicular pastry liability if they present themselves physically at any official unit event, practical training exercise, or live operational briefing driving a standard non-4x4 passenger vehicle.
(2) **The Non-Tactical Vehicle Catch-All:** For the strict execution of this section, a "Non-Tactical Vehicle" is defined as any ordinary front-wheel-drive or rear-wheel-drive hatchback, saloon, estate, or urban runabout entirely lacking a dual-range transfer box, all-terrain tires, or sufficient ground clearance to survive a damp field.
(3) **Statutory Operational Exemptions:** A Responder is completely exempt from culinary liability under this section if they meet either of the following conditions:
(a) **Prior Management Approval:** The use of a non-4x4 vehicle was explicitly discussed and previously agreed with the regional management or coordination team prior to the event, training night, or deployment; or
(b) **Known Terrain Suitability:** The specific assignment, venue, or role features a known and pre-vetted suitability that explicitly eliminates the need for an off-road capable vehicle (such as indoor classroom briefings, clean tarmac logistics desks, or hard-standing public events where a 4x4 asset would be entirely redundant).
(4) An infraction under this section is declared fully made out the moment a non-exempt driver—
(a) Parks a low-clearance hatchback prominently in a line of heavily modified, lifted response trucks, creating an immediate and severe visual mismatch;
(b) A teammate sneaks a photograph of the offending commuter vehicle and posts it directly into the team **WhatsApp** channel to alert the rest of the unit; or
(c) Is forced to ask a colleague for a lift across the actual muddy deployment grid because their unapproved personal commuter car would instantly become stuck in less than two inches of wet grass.
(5) **The Mitigating Circumstances Clause:** If a Responder does not have prior management sign-off but can provide clear, verifiable evidence via official **Email** or **WhatsApp** prior to arrival proving their main 4x4 asset is currently off the road undergoing urgent mechanical repairs, custom wiring upgrades, or a chassis replacement, the penalty may be mitigated by the group.
(6) Penalties for dropping the unit's off-road aesthetic without a valid exemption are scaled based on the level of visibility:
(a) Turning up to a standard indoor classroom session or committee meeting in an unapproved car: One (1) standard cake or treat per person present; or
(b) Turning up to a live, multi-agency field deployment, public event grid, or adverse weather standby in an unapproved car: A mandatory **Severe Aesthetic Demotion Ticket**, requiring premium bakery-fresh doughnuts scaling strictly to exactly one per person present to buy back operational respect.
