17. Media Lapses and Accidental Exposures

(1) This section establishes the culinary penalties for digital blunders, public-facing comment slip-ups, and instances where a volunteer is captured or highlighted by external media sources during an event or live deployment.
(2) **The Open-Ended Public Media Exposure Catch-All:** A Responder falls within the scope of this section if their operational conduct, digital replies, or vehicle identity becomes the subject of accidental public scrutiny. This explicitly includes:
(a) **The Public Comment / Post Flub:** Making an embarrassing typo, posting a misplaced emoji, or leaving a confusingly worded response while commenting on behalf of the group, or on an public thread related to a local event;
(b) **The Bystander Cam (Captured Without Knowledge):** Getting filmed, photographed, or live-streamed by a member of the public or an event spectator while performing a less-than-graceful action on-duty (such as tripping over a traffic cone, struggling to close a trailer door, or eating a messy burger at the logistics desk) and having it uploaded to local community spaces; or
(c) **The Local Community Mention Notification:** Getting explicitly singled out, named, or having your specific vehicle registration plate mentioned in a local Facebook group, Nextdoor post, or online forum thread regarding area deployments.
(3) **The Minor Mention Tariff:** If a volunteer or their truck receives a benign, neutral, or slightly humorous mention by name or vehicle profile in external communications, a **Statutory Minor Mention Fine** is immediately assessed.
(4) An offence under this section is fully established the moment a teammate spots the comment typo or links the external post into the team **WhatsApp** or **Email** streams to highlight the exposure.
(5) Penalties are scaled based on the level of public visibility and potential for unit banter:
(a) **The Minor Mention / Comment Slip:** For a basic public typo or a simple community group name drop that requires no management cleanup: One (1) standard cake or treat per person present to settle the minor mention tariff; or
(b) **The Full Digital Capture (Aggravated Exposure):** For getting caught on video by a bystander doing something undeniably funny or uncoordinated on a live event grid, resulting in widespread tracking across the team network: A mandatory penalty of premium bakery-fresh doughnuts scaling strictly to exactly one per person present to soothe the collective pride.
