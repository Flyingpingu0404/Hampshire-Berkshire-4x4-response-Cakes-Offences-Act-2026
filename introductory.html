Hampshire & Berkshire 4x4 Response Cakes (Offences) Act 2026

An Act to make provision for the handling, prosecution, and sugary penalisation of operational blunders, logistical lapses, administrative errors, and catastrophic vehicular groundings within the volunteer response framework; to clarify the boundaries of legitimate sugary restitution; and to preserve team morale and workplace harmony through mandatory pastry distribution.

[19th June 2026]

BE IT ENACTED by the absolute authority of the collective response unit, gathered in coordination rooms, active deployment sites, and around office kettles, by and with the advice and hungry consent of the operational majority, as follows:—

This statute stands as a binding framework for all personnel. By continuing to operate team assets, radio networks, and off-road machinery, all members acknowledge the supreme jurisdictional authority of the Court of Kettle Appeal and accept the penalty terms outlined herein without exception.
