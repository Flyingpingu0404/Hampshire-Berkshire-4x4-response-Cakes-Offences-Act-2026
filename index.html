APPENDIX A

For the purposes of the standard cake fine, it should be noted that 'Jaffa Cakes' either branded as such or differently, are not cakes for the purposes of this Act.
Mandated Scale of Penalty: Any culinary penalty allocated under this Act must strictly represent **one (1) individual cake, premium treat, or holiday item per person** active on the team's next deployment, event, or training session. Group-wide pool scaling to bypass individual headcount distribution is strictly illegal.
Cakes is understood to include any of the following (Standard Offence — 1 per person present); this list is not exhaustive—
(1) Cakes
(2) Muffins
(3) Cookies
(4) Doughnuts
(5) Slices
Cakes is understood to include any of the following in the instance of an aggravated offence (Premium Offence — 1 premium item per person present)—
(1) Krispy Kreme Doughnuts
(2) Cream Cakes
(3) Homemade / freshly baked cakes
(4) An overwhelming number of 'standard cakes' ensuring a minimum surplus layout.
Under Section 8, cakes as defined above can be replaced with an exciting treat from a holiday destination, scaling precisely to one per attendee.
