20. Social Event and Catering Infractions

(1) This section establishes the strict operational pastry fines for any volunteer who compromises group morale, mismanages essential unit refreshments, or fails to maintain social discipline during official team stand downs, club meetings, or catering-dependent briefings.
(2) **The Endless Hospitality Catch-All:** For the absolute avoidance of doubt, the provisions of this section apply to **any and all catering assets, food preparations, and social bookings** associated with the unit, completely irrespective of whether the event takes place in a muddy field staging site, the main clubhouse kitchen, a public pub, or a local festival bar tent.
(3) Hospitality and social "fuck-ups" that trigger an immediate cake liability under this universal framework include, but are not limited to—
(a) **The Sacrilegious Beverage Brew:** Making a round of tea or coffee for the team that is universally deemed undrinkable (including producing "dishwater tea," dropping biscuit crumbs into the mug, or forgetting the sugar allocations);
(b) **The Critical Milk / Ration Omission:** Arriving to a cold morning training exercise or an overnight standby deployment having volunteered to supply the base kit, but completely forgetting the milk, tea bags, or basic morning rations;
(c) **The Incinerated Briefing Breakfast:** Burning the bacon rolls, sausages, or outdoor catering supplies on the portable gas grill while managing the breakfast station, rendering the team's operational fuel completely charcoal-based; or
(d) **The No-Show Social Ghosting:** Confirming attendance for a pre-booked team dinner, pub carvery, or end-of-season social night, and then failing to show up without a valid emergency excuse, leaving the unit with an empty chair and a mismatched table booking.
(4) An infraction under this section is legally established the moment a teammate takes their first sip of terrible tea, peers into an empty cooler box, or drops a message into the **WhatsApp** or **Email** thread asking: *"Where are you? We've already ordered the starters."*
(5) Penalties are assessed based on the level of morale deflation caused to the unit:
(a) **The Minor Catering Slip:** For basic tea-making errors or forgetting non-essential snacks: One (1) standard supermarket sharing cake to restore group harmony; or
(b) **The Complete Social Desertion / Kitchen Burnout:** For burning the main shift breakfast, leaving the staging area entirely out of caffeine, or ghosting a pre-booked club table: A mandatory upgrade to a minimum of twelve (12) premium bakery-fresh doughnuts to buy back social standing.
