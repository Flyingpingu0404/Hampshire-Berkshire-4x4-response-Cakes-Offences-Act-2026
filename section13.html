13. Key Misplacement and Retention Cakes

(1) A Responder commits an operational locking or logistical infraction under this section if, upon finishing an active shift, event deployment, training night, or official social gathering, they improperly retain, transfer, or misplace any critical access key.
(2) **The Universal Key Catch-All (Open-Ended Extract):** For the absolute avoidance of doubt, the provisions of this section shall apply to the unauthorized retention, accidental pocketing, or incorrect relocation of **any and all keys** handled during or directly following a unit activity, completely irrespective of ownership. This explicitly includes:
(a) **Shared Team Assets:** Keys to a shared team support trailer, equipment lockbox, regional secure compound gate, or pooled response asset;
(b) **Third-Party Vehicles and Trailers:** Accidentally dropping or locking operational keys inside another Responder's personal vehicle, support truck, or support craft trailer; and
(c) **Personal and Residential Property:** Walking away with or misplacing the personal house keys, private car keys, or gate fobs belonging to another team member or volunteer following a vehicle-share, carpool, lift, or deployment briefing.
(3) The offence is declared fully absolute the exact moment an individual realizes they are locked out, an asset is grounded, or a frantic tracer message is broadcasted across **WhatsApp** or **Email** asking: *"Who has the keys?"*
(4) Because pocketing or misplacing keys directly disrupts personal lives and responses, penalties under this section are categorized as **High-Tier Operational Evasions**:
(a) **The Turn-Around Clause:** If the culprit realizes their mistake within 15 minutes of leaving, turns their vehicle around, and returns the keys immediately, the penalty stands at a standard full-sized supermarket cake; or
(b) **The Grounded Property Aggravation:** If the culprit makes it all the way home, requiring a secondary volunteer to drive out to track down the keys, or leaving a truck, house, or trailer completely stranded until a later date, the penalty automatically escalates to a minimum of twelve (12) premium bakery-fresh doughnuts plus a mandatory round of bacon rolls for the affected party.
